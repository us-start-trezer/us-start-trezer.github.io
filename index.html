Trezor.io/start is the official setup guide for users who have purchased a Trezor hardware wallet. This webpage provides step-by-step instructions to help users configure their devices securely, ensuring that their cryptocurrencies are well-protected.

Getting Started

  1. Unboxing and Connecting: Upon receiving your Trezor device, visit trezor.io/start. Begin by unboxing the device and connecting it to your computer or mobile device using the provided USB cable.
  2. Firmware Installation: When you first connect your Trezor, it may require a firmware update. Follow the prompts to install the latest firmware version. This ensures your device is equipped with the most recent security features.
  3. Setting Up a New Wallet: After the firmware installation, you’ll be prompted to create a new wallet. The Trezor device will generate a recovery seed—a series of 12, 18, or 24 words. This seed is crucial for recovering your wallet in case the device is lost or damaged. Write down the seed on a physical piece of paper and store it securely.
  4. PIN and Passphrase Setup: To add an additional layer of security, set up a PIN code on your Trezor. Optionally, you can also set up a passphrase. The passphrase is an extra word or phrase added to the seed, which enhances security but should be remembered carefully, as it cannot be recovered.
  5. Installing Trezor Suite: Trezor Suite is the official desktop and web application that allows you to manage your cryptocurrencies. Download and install Trezor Suite from trezor.io/start. Once installed, you can use the application to send, receive, and monitor your crypto assets.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is a recovery seed?
The recovery seed is a sequence of 12, 18, or 24 words generated by your Trezor device. It is the backup of your entire wallet and is essential for recovery in case of device loss or failure. Store it securely and never share it with anyone.

2. What if I lose my recovery seed?
If you lose your recovery seed and your Trezor device becomes inaccessible, you will not be able to recover your cryptocurrencies. It’s vital to store your seed in a secure, offline location.

3. How can I reset my Trezor device?
You can reset your Trezor by selecting the "Wipe Device" option in Trezor Suite. This will erase all data from the device, including the PIN and wallet, allowing you to start fresh with a new setup.

4. Is Trezor Suite necessary?
Yes, Trezor Suite is the primary interface for managing your Trezor wallet. It provides a secure environment to interact with your crypto assets, including sending, receiving, and checking balances.

5. Can I use my Trezor without Trezor Suite?
While Trezor Suite is recommended for the best user experience, you can use your Trezor with third-party applications like Electrum, Mycelium, and others, which support Trezor devices.
